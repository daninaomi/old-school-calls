What users do ?

The aim of this project is connect students eager to practice new languages with seniors in retirement homes all around the world. “User” is the interactive mode for those seeking to learn a language, teach one or only meet and talk with other people.

Student

Focused on the “speaking” part of language learning, you will be able to connect with elders from all over the world ! Search for the language you want to practice, a retirement home, see if is there anyone available and start talking ! If you are still insecure, you can start with some of our online exercises.

Senior

Meet and connect with students of all ages from all over the world ! Search for the language you speak, see if is there anyone available and start talking ! If you are part of, or know someone part of a retirement home which is not presented on the list, contact us!
